Ideal Job
Picture 1: Chief Technology Officer
Chief Technology Officer (CTO)     Link to JobAd
Description
Heading up an international operation, this strategic role requires a person who has demonstrated success in similar roles and will be responsible for the architecture of public facing consumer websites focussed on the automotive industry.
What appeals to me about this job
This role would be the natural progression into the top senior IT leadership roles within the market. I like that it is not solely managerial but contains elements of technical input and forward-thinking in the areas of DevOps & Cloud. Additionally, it is a role where there is involvement with those outside of IT; particularly in Operations and Marketing.
Skills, Qualifications and Experience Required
Academic wise, an IT or Engineering discpline degree coupled with DevOps exposure and previous technical leadership roles. Particular focus on exposure to Amazon cloud (AWS) and awareness of industry trends and innovation. Understanding and knowledge of working with teams in remote settings and delegation capability.
Skills, Qualifications and Experiences I have
Having been in the IT industry for quite a while, I feel that I can offer a lot in this type of role. I come from a strong Infrastructure background and have held many technical leadership positions across multinational companies. I hold many technical qualifications from known vendors such as Microsoft, Cisco, Citrix and VMware and have over 10 years experience in leading, running and managing projects including budgetary responsibility.
My plan
Once I have finished my degree with RMIT (expected early in 2020) I will have the academic rounding to close off the academic demands of the role. My experience with DevOps particularly in the development arena that RMIT has provided me will address the gaps in that space. Finally I am also undergoing AWS technical training to better improve my understanding and experience of Amazon's cloud offering.
